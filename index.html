About Lok Namah Group Pvt. Ltd.

With over 25 years of industry experience, Lok Namah Group Pvt. Ltd. was founded by an entrepreneur - Lokesh Gupta, who has worked at the ground level and truly understands customer needs. Built on years of knowledge and hands-on expertise, we launched our own brand to deliver high-quality sanitaryware, bathware, tiles, kitchen sinks, faucets, and allied products.

We focus on superior design, lasting quality, and customer satisfaction. Every product reflects our passion for excellence and our goal to enhance everyday living. At Lok Namah, we don’t just meet expectations—we aim to exceed them.
